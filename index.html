Local Favorites

What We're Known For

Fresh, homemade dishes that keep our regulars coming back every week.

Breakfast Tacos

Fresh tortillas filled with eggs, chorizo, bacon, potato, or barbacoa. A local favorite and our most popular item.

Carne Guisada

Tender beef simmered in a savory tomato and pepper gravy. Rich, comforting, and served with fresh tortillas.

Huevos Rancheros

Fried eggs over corn tortillas smothered in our house ranchero sauce. A hearty, traditional breakfast plate.

Chicken Enchiladas

Rolled corn tortillas stuffed with seasoned chicken, topped with our house enchilada sauce and melted cheese.

Beef Fajitas

Sizzling beef with grilled peppers and onions, served with warm tortillas, rice, beans, and all the fixings.

Green Salsa & Tortillas

Our famous homemade green salsa paired with fresh-made tortillas. The combination everyone asks about.

Our Story

A Neighborhood Favorite in College Station

For years, Coco Loco has been a favorite neighborhood spot for authentic Mexican food in College Station. Known for homemade tortillas, flavorful green salsa, generous portions, and friendly service, Coco Loco is where locals come when they want food that feels homemade.

Whether you're a student grabbing breakfast tacos before class, a family enjoying a weekend brunch, or a regular who knows everyone by name — this is your place.

"The kind of food that makes you feel at home."
